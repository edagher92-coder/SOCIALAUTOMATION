AdPilot OS

A read-only operating system for paid ads. It audits your Meta & TikTok ad accounts, scores a 0–100 Campaign Health Score, and proposes safe, numbers-first fixes — it never edits, pauses, creates, or spends on a live ad without your explicit approval. It proposes; the human approves.
v6.0.0 · Australian English & AUD · 713 automated tests green · Next.js 16 · React 19 · Supabase (RLS) · Stripe

1 Campaign Health Score

0–100, computed from 13 weighted factors. Factors with no data redistribute their weight to the rest, so the score stays fair. Four bands set the next action:

GREEN≥ 80
YELLOW≥ 60
ORANGE≥ 40
RED< 40

Green = scale-eligible (if tracking is clean) · Yellow = fix weak factors first · Orange = act this week · Red = stop & fix before spending more.

2 Per-ad decisions — seven verdicts

Every ad gets exactly one verdict. Scale and kill only fire when a Wilson significance test confirms the result — never on a noisy sample. Each verdict carries a safe, reversible proposal.

SCALECPA ≤ break-even, health ≥ 70, tracking clean, win significant → +≤20% budget & duplicate
KILLCPA > 1.5× break-even, loss significant → pause & reallocate
REDUCECPA moderately high → cut budget, test a fresh angle
REFRESHFrequency ≥ 4 with falling or low CTR → 3–5 fresh variants
FIX-TRACKINGSpend with zero/implausible results → audit pixel / events / CAPI first
KEEPWithin range → monitor, no change
INSUFFICIENT< 50 clicks & < 15 conversions → let spend accumulate

Lead-gen uses a break-even-CPL split (optional lead_close_rate); never auto-kills on leads.

🔒 The read-only invariant — proposes, never acts on its own

AdPilot is read-only by default: it never edits, pauses, creates, or spends on a live ad. Acting on a live ad requires an explicit typed “YES”, is Expert-tier only, and is double-gated by the environment flag ADS_WRITE_ENABLED — which ships OFF. Every engine decision is marked safe; all connections use read-only API scopes; numbers-first, anti-hype — no guarantees and no financial, legal, medical or tax advice.

4 Statistical diagnostics NEW · V6

  • Change-point creative-fatigue onset — pinpoints the day engagement stepped down (falling hold-rate as a leading signal) with a confidence label, before performance craters.
  • Account-level anomaly scan — robust median/MAD z-score flags harmful spikes & drops with watch/alert severity.
  • purchase_roas reconciliation — Meta’s attribution-window ROAS shown beside derived ROAS as an honest cross-check.
  • Live ingestion + Data Sync panel — append-only audit of every pull (ok / partial / rate-limited), rows, API version, time.

5 Automation & safety NEW · V6

  • Configurable rules engine — absolute + rolling-baseline (z-score / WoW) rules emit alerts & proposals; a min-volume gate kills false alarms. Never executes.
  • “What to test next” recommender — ICE-ranked, propose-only test backlog: one-variable hypothesis, primary metric, budget, 7–14d read-out, paused-duplicate setup.
  • AI usage & cost telemetry — per-call tokens & AUD cost, best-effort.
  • AI eval harness — deterministic safety guards + adversarial fixtures gate the read-only invariant in CI.

6 Reports & delivery

  • Five report kinds — daily, weekly, monthly briefs + first-look Meta & TikTok audits.
  • Deterministic tables for every number; Riley writes the plain-English prose only.
  • Optional projected-impact section forecasting a proposed change.
  • Scheduled PDF delivery — the weekly digest emails the report PDF (via Resend); Drive/Gmail delivery owner-gated.

7 Connect & data

  • Read-only connect — Meta (ads_read, read_insights) & TikTok (ads.read) OAuth, a pasted System User token, or CSV import (free).
  • 30-day sync window; rate-limit backoff & per-account resilience (fail-closed).
  • Tokens encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM); every table is RLS-scoped to org members.
  • One-click “Run my first audit” after connecting.
