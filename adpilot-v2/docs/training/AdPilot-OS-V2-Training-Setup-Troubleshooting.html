The golden rule. AdPilot is read-only. It scores your campaigns and writes plain-English proposals. You decide and you make any change inside Meta/TikTok. Nothing here can spend money or alter a live ad.

Part 1 — Training

1.1 What AdPilot OS does

AdPilot turns raw Meta & TikTok ad data into three things a busy operator actually needs:

  • A Campaign Health Score (0–100) — one number that tells you if the account is healthy.
  • Plain-English findings — what's working, what's bleeding money, and why.
  • Prioritised, safe recommendations — keep / scale / reduce / refresh / fix-tracking / kill — always as proposals.

It works for a single business or a whole agency: every client lives in its own isolated workspace.

1.2 Beginner vs Advanced mode

ModeWho it's forWhat you see
BeginnerOwners, new staffGuided, plain-English view — the score, the headline issues, and the next best actions.
AdvancedMedia buyers, analystsEverything in Beginner plus the full 13-factor breakdown (“Why this score?”), per-factor weights and contributions.

Switch any time with the toggle in the top-left of the sidebar. Your choice is remembered.

1.3 The Health Score explained

The score is a weighted average of 13 factors, mapped to a colour band:

BandScoreWhat it means
Green80–100Healthy. Look for scale opportunities.
Yellow60–79Workable, but fix the flagged issues.
Orange40–59Underperforming. Act this week.
Red0–39Urgent. Money is likely being wasted.

The 13 factors

Tracking quality · CPA vs break-even · spend efficiency · conversion rate · click-through rate (CTR) · lead quality · creative freshness (fatigue) · cost per click (CPC) · naming convention · offer strength · landing-page alignment · budget pacing · data confidence.

Data confidence is the honesty factor — if there isn't enough spend/conversions to trust the result, AdPilot says so instead of pretending. In Advanced mode, open “Why this score?” to see each factor's score, weight, and points contributed.

1.4 Your daily / weekly workflow

  1. Sign in → land on Ads Health.
  2. Get data in — Sync connected accounts, or paste/upload a CSV.
  3. Confirm economics in Settings (average sale value + gross margin → defines break-even).
  4. Analyse → read the score, findings and proposals.
  5. Act in Meta/TikTok on the proposals you agree with.
  6. Save / send — open the report → Download branded PDF for the client.

1.5 Reading the results

  • Score hero — the gauge, the band, and a one-line verdict.
  • Key metrics — Spend, CPA, break-even CPA, ROAS, break-even ROAS, MER, leads, purchases.
  • Findings — severity-tagged (Critical → Info) issues with a short explanation.
  • Proposals — per-campaign verdict, the reason, and the exact suggested action.

1.6 Recommendations & safety

VerdictMeaning
keepPerforming to target — leave it running.
scaleStrong & healthy — consider increasing budget (only suggested when health ≥ 70 and tracking is clean).
reduceSlipping — trim budget / tighten targeting.
refreshCreative fatigue — rotate in new creative.
fix-trackingConversions/pixel look broken — fix before judging performance.
killWasting spend with no path to profit — pause it.
insufficient-dataNot enough signal yet — let it run / gather more.
Every verdict is a proposal. AdPilot never makes the change for you.

1.7 Creative library — link or upload

Bring AI audio, video and images from any tool — Midjourney, Sora, Runway, Pika, ElevenLabs, Canva, HeyGen, or your own files:

  • Link — paste the asset URL (the universal method; works with every creator tool).
  • Upload — store your own files in private storage.

AdPilot organises assets, links them to campaigns, and feeds them into creative briefs. You bring the content — AdPilot does the rest.

1.8 Multi-client (agency)

Use the Client selector (top-left) to switch workspaces, or ➕ Add client… to create one. Each client is fully isolated — data, connections, reports and branding never cross over (enforced by row-level security in the database).

1.9 Reports & one-click branded PDF

Every analysis auto-saves under Saved Reports. Open one → Download branded PDF. Set your agency logo, colour and support email under White-label so client reports carry your brand.

1.10 Notifications & alerts

Under Notifications: set an email, then enable the weekly digest and critical alerts (CPA blowouts, broken tracking, creative fatigue, Red health). A daily scheduled job scores connected accounts and emails any breaches. Use Send test email to confirm delivery.

Part 2 — Setup

2.1 Deployment essentials (owner / admin)

AdPilot V2 runs on Vercel (the app) + Supabase (database, auth, storage). Minimum to go live:

  1. Create a Supabase project; run the SQL migrations in adpilot-v2/supabase/migrations (through 0004_hardening.sql).
  2. Deploy adpilot-v2 to Vercel; set the environment variables (see §2.6).
  3. Set the two required keys: NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL and NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY. Everything else is optional and degrades gracefully.
Before going live: rotate any previously-shared API keys (Anthropic, Stripe), set a real SUPPORT_EMAIL, and generate a fresh TOKEN_ENCRYPTION_KEY and CRON_SECRET.

2.2 Three ways to bring data in

Pick what suits you — you can mix them. All paths are read-only.

MethodBest forBenefitsDrawbacks
CSV uploadAnyone, instantZero setup, no permissions, fully private, works offlineManual; a point-in-time snapshot, not live
API token (OAuth)Most users / auto-syncOne-click connect, scheduled auto-sync + scoring + alerts, least-privilege read scopes, tokens encrypted at restRequires a platform app configured once; tokens expire & refresh; app review for production
MCP serverPower users / Claude-drivenDrive it from Claude/agents, scriptable, great for multi-tool orchestrationMore moving parts; you host the MCP; less turnkey
Recommendation: API token (OAuth) for daily auto-sync, CSV as the always-available fallback, MCP if you already orchestrate via Claude.

2.3 Option A — CSV (no setup)

Export at ad level with a daily breakdown from Meta Ads Manager / TikTok Ads Manager, then paste or upload on the Ads Health page. Columns auto-map to the universal schema (see Appendix B). Keep the header row.

2.4 Option B — API token (OAuth) — recommended

Server env keys (set once)

# Meta
META_APP_ID=...
META_APP_SECRET=...
# TikTok
TIKTOK_APP_ID=...
TIKTOK_APP_SECRET=...
# shared
OAUTH_REDIRECT_BASE=https://your-app.vercel.app   # no trailing slash
TOKEN_ENCRYPTION_KEY=...   # node -e "console.log(require('crypto').randomBytes(32).toString('base64'))"

Meta (Facebook / Instagram)

  1. developers.facebook.com → Create App → type Business → add Marketing API.
  2. App settings → Basic: copy App ID + App secret → META_APP_ID / META_APP_SECRET.
  3. Facebook Login → Settings → Valid OAuth redirect URIs: add https://your-app.vercel.app/api/oauth/meta/callback.
  4. Scopes: ads_read, read_insights (request in App Review for production; dev mode works for your own accounts).
  5. In AdPilot: Connect Accounts → Connect Meta → approve → ad accounts appear → Sync now.

TikTok Ads

  1. business-api.tiktok.com → My Apps → create an app; enable Reporting / Ads (read).
  2. Copy App ID + Secret → TIKTOK_APP_ID / TIKTOK_APP_SECRET.
  3. Redirect URI: https://your-app.vercel.app/api/oauth/tiktok/callback.
  4. Scope: ads.read.
  5. In AdPilot: Connect Accounts → Connect TikTok → approve → Sync now.
Tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage and are never sent to the browser. Scheduled sync & scoring run via the daily cron once connected.

2.5 Option C — MCP server (Claude-driven)

If you run Meta/TikTok through an MCP server, have your agent export insights to the universal schema and push them to POST /api/ingest (authenticated with a session, or a server x-api-key = INGEST_API_KEY plus an organisation_id). It can also score and save in the same call. The schema is documented in docs/MCP-INGEST.md.

3.2 Diagnostics

  • Liveness: /api/health. Host status: Vercel dashboard.
  • Engine integrity: a self-test runs on every build, confirming parity with the reference Python engine (30/30 checks).
  • Bad score? Open “Why this score?”, check data confidence, and verify the CSV columns.
  • Connection issues? Re-check Connect Accounts; reconnect expired tokens.

3.3 Common issues & fixes

3.4 FAQ

  • Does it change my ads? No — read-only, proposals only.
  • Is my data private? CSVs are processed in-browser; connected data is org-isolated (RLS); tokens are encrypted.
  • Meta + TikTok together? Yes — one schema, one combined score plus per-platform splits.
  • Do I need to code? No.
  • Results guaranteed? No — it surfaces issues and opportunities; performance depends on your execution.

3.5 How to report an error (so it gets fixed fast)

Email your support contact (SUPPORT_EMAIL) with:

  1. What you did (the steps).
  2. What you expected vs what actually happened.
  3. The exact error text (screenshot is ideal).
  4. Which client/workspace you were in.
  5. A small sample of the CSV columns (no need for full data).

For developers: check Vercel & Supabase logs; the build self-test confirms engine integrity.

Appendix

A. Glossary

TermMeaning
CPACost per acquisition — spend ÷ conversions.
Break-even CPAAverage sale value × gross margin. Above it, you lose money per sale.
ROASReturn on ad spend — revenue ÷ spend.
Break-even ROAS1 ÷ gross margin.
MERMarketing efficiency ratio — total revenue ÷ total ad spend.
CTR / CPC / CPMClick-through rate / cost per click / cost per 1,000 impressions.
Creative fatiguePerformance decay as an audience sees the same creative too often.
RLSRow-level security — database rules that isolate each client's data.

B. Universal CSV schema

Columns auto-map; these are the canonical names. Missing columns are handled — provide what you have.

business_name, platform, campaign_name, date,
spend, impressions, reach, clicks,
leads, purchases, revenue,
three_second_views, thruplays, lead_quality_score,
utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, tracking_status

C. Break-even maths

  • Break-even CPA = average sale value × gross margin
  • Break-even ROAS = 1 ÷ gross margin

Example: $200 sale × 0.60 margin → break-even CPA $120; break-even ROAS 1.67×.
