## Ad structure ("engines")
Spend is consolidated into a few funded "engines" rather than thinly spread ad sets:
- **Warm Retargeting (Click-to-WhatsApp)** — the cheapest, highest-intent engine; the documented
  "engine to carry the month". Keep it ON; its ceiling is warm-audience size.
- **EOFY / seasonal machine sales** — high-ticket, time-limited; run to its end date, don't scale;
  build the NSW-wide successor (avoid Sydney-only geo + junk audience filters).

  conversations after a fair test (≥3 days), the fix is the **creative/offer**, not budget:
  use the **"machine down → same-day Sydney call-out"** urgency angle.

## Targets & how to read it
- Optimise CTWA on **conversations started**, not link CTR.
- **<$8 per conversation** = healthy; **>$8** = watch-flag; **~$67.50** = qualified-lead reference.
- Quality signals: **reply rate ~70%+** and **depth-3+ replies** (real intent).
- Funding floor to exit Meta learning: **~$40–45/day per ad set** (~50 events/wk). Don't judge a
  starved ad set (<~$3/day) on volume — fund first, then test.
