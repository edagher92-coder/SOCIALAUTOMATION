## AUD / GST Notes

1. **GST registration:** If AdPilot OS is sold from an entity registered for GST (ABN + GST registration required above $75,000 AUD annual turnover), add 10% GST to all prices for Australian customers.

2. **Platforms like Gumroad and Lemon Squeezy:** These platforms may collect and remit GST automatically for digital products sold to Australian buyers under the Australian Tax Office's "non-resident digital service" rules. Confirm with your accountant and your platform's tax settings before launch.

3. **International pricing:** If selling in USD, apply an approximate conversion at time of pricing (e.g., AUD $197 ≈ USD $125–135 at 2026 rates). Consider keeping USD pricing slightly lower to account for currency risk.

4. **Invoice requirements:** For Agency tier ($1,997+), issue a proper tax invoice showing ABN, GST amount (if applicable), and line item descriptions. Required for business buyers claiming input tax credits.

5. **Stripe / Lemon Squeezy merchant of record:** Using a platform as merchant of record (Lemon Squeezy does this) simplifies international tax handling significantly — worth considering for the Agency tier.
