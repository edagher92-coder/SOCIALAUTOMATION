---

## Executive Summary

Example Co spent $7,841 in May across Meta and TikTok, generating 152 leads at an average CPL of $51.59 and 18 purchases at an average CPA of $435.61. Revenue attributed to paid ads was $43,200, delivering an 8-week ROAS of 5.51 and MER of 5.51 against a break-even ROAS of 2.86 (35% gross margin, $2,500 average sale).

Paid advertising was profitable in May. The retargeting campaigns performed strongly throughout the month. Cold audience campaigns underperformed in the second half due to creative fatigue — two new creatives launched in the final week are early positive signals. TikTok contributed 38% of leads but 0% of directly attributed revenue — a tracking and attribution issue was identified and is being resolved in June.

**Monthly verdict:** Profitable. CPA 50% below break-even. One structural issue (TikTok attribution) and one creative refresh cycle needed heading into June.
