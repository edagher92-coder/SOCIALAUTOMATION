Frequently asked questions

Does my ad data get uploaded anywhere?

No. All analysis runs locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.

Can it change my campaigns?

No — it's read-only. It produces proposals; you decide and act in Ads Manager. Budget changes always require your explicit confirmation.

How is the Health Score calculated?

A weighted average of 13 factors (tracking, CPA vs break-even, CTR, creative fatigue, conversion rate, lead quality and more). Run an analysis and open "Why this score?" to see the full breakdown for your data.

What can I export?

A CSV (summary, campaigns, findings, proposals) and a Print / Save-as-PDF client-ready report.
